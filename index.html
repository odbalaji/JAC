About JAC ( JCI Alumni Club) - An Initiative from JCI India

The JCI Alumni Club (JAC) of JCI India is not just a club; it's a dynamic community of JCI India graduates who are dedicated to making a positive impact both in their local communities and beyond. Comprised of esteemed senior members of Junior Chamber International (JCI) India, JAC's primary mission is to connect JCI alumni and foster the continual growth and development of the JCI movement

As a member of JAC, you're not just joining a club; you're becoming a part of a vibrant community where collaboration thrives. Engage in innovative projects, share your knowledge and expertise, and connect with like-minded individuals through avenues such as fellowship, travel, cultural exploration, and business networking. JAC is not just an alumni club, it's a support system that values your overall wellbeing, providing guidance in areas such as wealth management and personal development.

Through our diverse events and activities, you can reminisce about cherished memories, forge new connections, and build lasting friendships. Joining JAC means becoming a part of a community committed to creating positive change and supporting each other along the way. Our community is here to help you achieve our shared vision and make a meaningful impact. Join us and be a part of a community that is making a difference

Our Vision

To be the foremost global network of young leaders.

Our Mission

To provide leadership development opportunities that empower young people to create positive change.

New Membership

JCI Alumni Club will be a nationwide body of all senior members. This forum will work under the supervision and guidance of the National Governing Board. It shall endeavor to support the membership growth of JCI India, from time to time and will function under the National Governing Board with governing rules.

Given below are the criteria to join JCI Alumni Club;

Any individual with more than 40 years of age can apply
Former member of JCI India, who has been as a member of any local JCI organization
Membership is by application only
An annual fee or life membership fee is payable on application

Message from Our National Chairman

Dear Esteemed JAC Members,
Greetings to you!

As I begin my journey as the National Chairman of JAC 2024, I eagerly look forward to the exciting path that lies ahead. Our mission is to pave the way for significant change, embodied in the four pillars of Reconnecting, Recognising, Rejoicing, and Reflecting

At the forefront of our priorities is the cultivation of Reconnection among our diverse graduates, fortifying the bonds that unite our JAC community. It is imperative that we celebrate the achievements of our distinguished alumni, acknowledging the profound impact they have on the organisation and the society at large.

JAC remains steadfast in its commitment to fostering positive change by addressing societal concerns and actively participating in cultural activities. As a cohesive team, our objective is to stride forward, aiming for transformative achievements with 10,000 members and substantial contributions to society as our next milestones.

As we embark on this collective journey, I extend my heartfelt wishes for a year filled with Mr meaningful accomplishments. Together, let us collaboratively shape the future of JAC.
