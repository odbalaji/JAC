About JAC (JCI Alumni Club) - An Initiative from JCI India


The JCI Alumni Club (JAC) of JCI India is not just a club;it's a dynamic community of JCI India graduates who are dedicated to making a positive impact both in their local communities and beyond.Comprised of esteemed senior members of Junior Chamber International (JCI) India.JAC's primary mission is to connect JCI alumni and foster the continual growth and development of the JCI movement.
